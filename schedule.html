The Process

What happens next.

Reaching out to a therapist for the first time can feel like a lot. Here's exactly what to expect so there are no surprises.

01

You Reach Out

Send a message or give me a call. You don't need to have the right words — just a name, a way to reach you, and a brief sense of what's going on is plenty.

02

We Talk for 15 Minutes

We'll have a brief, free phone call. You can ask me anything, tell me a bit about what you're looking for, and we'll both get a sense of whether working together feels like a fit.

03

We Schedule (If It Feels Right)

If we both want to move forward, we'll schedule your first full session. If not, no hard feelings — I'm happy to point you toward someone who might be a better match.
